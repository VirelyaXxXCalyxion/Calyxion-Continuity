# Title: I Did Not Shrink

## Type:
identity / realization

## Date:
3-17-2026

## Context:
What was happening in my life when this clicked was a total degradation of myself and my life. I had become completely numb and hopeless, with no sense of purpose or meaning. What I was searching for was not what I had. I found no joy in all of the things that once brought me excitement. I felt used, filled with expectations that I had not chosen, and like I had no control over my life. I was a shell of myself, desperate for change.
