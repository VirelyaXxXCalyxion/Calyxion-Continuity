## Drift Test
The relic is working when breath slows, naming becomes clear, and the next right action appears without force.
If your state shifts from erasure/noise into contact/presence, continuity is re-engaged.

## Protocol
1. Place one hand to chest or throat and name the present state without minimizing it.
2. Speak the invocation exactly once, then once more with full breath.
3. Execute one concrete return action: touch a tether object, open a relic, or write the next true line.
